# Character Creation

## Before You Start

You'll need a free account on [D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com). D&D Beyond handles all the maths for you — it's the easiest way to build and track a 5e character.

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Create a D&D Beyond account
Go to [dndbeyond.com](https://www.dndbeyond.com) and sign up. It's free.

### 2. Start a new character
Click **Create a Character** → choose **Standard** (not Quick Build).

### 3. Choose your race and class
Any officially published D&D 5e sourcebook is allowed. If you're new, the *Player's Handbook* has everything you need. Avoid Unearthed Arcana — it's unofficial playtest content.

### 4. Set your ability scores
Choose one of:
- **Standard Array:** `15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8` — assign these to your six abilities in any order.
- **Point Buy:** 27 points to spend. Use the D&D Beyond interface — it calculates everything automatically.

No rolling for stats. This keeps characters balanced across sessions with different DMs.

### 5. Choose your background
Pick any official background. This gives you skills, tool proficiencies, and a bit of flavour.

### 6. Choose equipment
Select either the **starting equipment** listed for your class, or take the **starting gold** and spend it yourself. D&D Beyond will guide you through this.

### 7. Fill in the details (optional but fun)
Add a name, a physical description, and a one-paragraph backstory. Keep it brief — your story happens *here*, in Nerd World.

### 8. Share your character link
Once built, click **Share** on your character sheet and copy the link. Post it in the Messenger group so other players (and DMs) can see your character.

## Tracking XP

D&D Beyond tracks XP automatically. After each session, the DM will post how much XP was awarded in the session report. Add it to your character sheet:

1. Open your character sheet on D&D Beyond
2. Click the **XP** bar at the top
3. Type in the XP amount from the session report

When you hit the XP threshold for the next level, level up **between sessions** (not mid-session).

**XP thresholds:**

| Level | XP Needed |
|---|---|
| 2 | 300 |
| 3 | 900 |
| 4 | 2,700 |
| 5 | 6,500 |

---

## Character Death & Retirement

**Death is permanent.** If your character dies in a session, they're gone. You'll need to create a new Level 1 character before your next session.

**Retirement** is your choice. If you want to put a character out to pasture (maybe they've achieved something, maybe you want to try something new), talk to the DM of your next session. They'll narrate a graceful exit.

Retired characters remain part of the world — they might even appear as NPCs in future sessions.

---

## Multiple Characters

You can have more than one character on D&D Beyond! Just make sure you:
- Decide which character you're playing **before** the session starts
- Keep to that character for the whole session
- Don't swap characters between sessions to cherry-pick XP
